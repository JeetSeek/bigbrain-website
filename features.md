BoilerBrain operates on a tiered subscription model, providing value to both heating professionals and homeowners through specialized AI-powered services.

### Subscription Tiers

1. **Free Tier**
   - Limited access to AI chat assistant
   - Basic troubleshooting support
   - Limited manual searches
   - Perfect for occasional users or those evaluating the service

2. **Plus Tier**
   - Full access to AI chat assistant
   - Comprehensive troubleshooting support
   - Unlimited manual searches
   - 24/7 support ticket system
   - Ideal for homeowners and DIY enthusiasts

3. **Pro Tier**
   - Everything in Plus
   - Priority support responses
   - Advanced technical diagnostics
   - API access for integration with business systems
   - Bulk manual downloads
   - Designed for professional heating engineers and service companies

### Revenue Streams

- **Subscription Fees**: Primary revenue from monthly/annual subscriptions
- **Premium Support**: Additional fees for urgent or specialized support
- **White-Label Solutions**: Custom branded versions for heating companies
- **API Access**: Enterprise integrations for larger HVAC businesses

### Target Market

- **Primary**: Professional heating engineers and HVAC technicians
- **Secondary**: Homeowners with technical knowledge
- **Tertiary**: Property managers and building maintenance teams
